\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{class_this}{This}} class represents cameras in game. 

The size of a map may be larger than the size of window. We use a camera to render part of the map. \mbox{\hyperlink{class_camera}{Camera}} follows the position of hero. It tries to render the hero at the center of the screen horizontally and one thirds from the bottom of screen vertically.
