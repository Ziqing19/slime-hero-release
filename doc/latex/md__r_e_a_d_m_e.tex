\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}{}\doxysection{How To Play}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}

\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Press left/right arrows or A/D on your keyboard to move your slime character.
\item Press S can split your slime. \mbox{\hyperlink{class_this}{This}} will make you {\itshape lighter}. Slime can only split with states of {\itshape Large} and {\itshape Middle}.
\item Collide with {\itshape other slimes} to aggregate to become {\itshape heavier}.
\item Collide with {\itshape enemies} will force your slime to split. If you cannot split, you will die.
\item You can jump over enemies\textquotesingle{} heads to destroy them.
\item You can collide with the {\itshape Flags} in the level to move to the next level. There are three levels in the current version of the game.
