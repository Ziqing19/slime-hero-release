Compilation Instructions

Make sure that the SDL2 development library and runtime library are installed correctly in your laptop.

cd Game/
python build.py
cd bin/
./platfomer // for mac
platformer.exe // for win

How To Play

  1. Press left/right arrows or A/D on your keyboard to move your slime character.
  2. Press S can split your slime. This will make you lighter. Slime can only split with states of Large and Middle.
  3. Collide with other slimes to aggregate to become heavier.
  4. Collide with enemies will force your slime to split. If you cannot split, you will die.
  5. You can jump over enemies' heads to destroy them.
  6. You can collide with the Flags in the level to move to the next level. There are three levels in the current version of the game.
